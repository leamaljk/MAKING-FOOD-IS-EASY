Lyonnaise Salad

SERVES 4

My interpretation of a classic salad. It's perfect for lunch—and the braised bacon also makes a great snack.

For the salad:

  • Slices country style bread (approx. ½ inch)
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Olive oil, as needed
  • Sea salt, as needed
  • Seasonal greens, as needed

For the Maple Braised Bacon:

See page XX

For the Warm Bacon Vin:

  • 4 pieces sliced bacon
  • ¼ cup yellow onion, small diced
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 small shallot, minced
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 2 tbsp dark brown sugar
  • 1 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 2 cups olive oil
  • 1 sprig of fresh thyme
  • Kosher salt, as needed
  • Black pepper, as needed

For the poached eggs:

  • 4 fresh large eggs, room temp
  • 4 cups water
  • ½ cup apple cider vinegar
  • Sea salt, as needed
  • Black pepper, as needed
  • Chives, shaved, as needed

You'll also need:

  • Food processor or stick blender
